What is Legend of Arcadia (ARCA) ?

What is Legend of Arcadia (ARCA) ? Legend of Arcadia is a multi-chain, free-to-play, and play-to-earn casual strategy card game that combines traditional gaming experience with Web3 GameFi 2.0. In addition to experiencing the game's immersive universe, players can also boost yield through staking, battling, and mining.

The fascinating universe of LOA is centered around a fictional colony of small, toy-like, humanoid creatures resembling toys (henceforth 'toy heroes' , in-game playable NFT characters). The aim of the game is to collect and raise different kinds of toy heroes (8 classes and 6 factions of toy heroes in total) and earn rewards through gameplay.
